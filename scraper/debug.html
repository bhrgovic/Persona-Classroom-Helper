Persona 4 Golden Answers: school class quiz, classroom test and exam questions and solutions

by RPG Site Staff on 25 June, 2020

As Mr. Morooka tells you early on in Persona 4 Golden, popularity leads to intimacy. One way to get popular quick in Persona 4 is to do well on the school class quiz questions and exams - and lucky for you we’ve got a full set of test answers to help you cheat your way to the top.

Advertisement. Keep scrolling for more

This page is nice and simple. P4G asks you to split your time between murder mystery antics in the back side of the TV and life as a high school student - and that means midterms, exams and pesky school quiz questions to answer. This page is pretty simple: we list all of the school questions and their answers so you can ace every test, all game, making all those times you're called on in Inaba High's classroom a breeze.

Persona 4 Golden Class Questions & Answers: all classroom quiz and test solutions

Getting questions right will often offer a social stat boost, such as to your knowledge, and when you’re helping out a friend you’ll also get a bonus to their Social Link. Doing well in school also carries other rewards once exams roll around. Remember that school tests are just one of the stat-boosting activities you can do in the day - you can also have a boxed lunch at break time, for instance.

ere they are below, listed in the order you'll encounter them - all the classroom questions, pop quiz moments and the like you'll encounter. We also cover all of the exams you face in Persona 4 Golden as well, of course. Getting these questions wrong won't hurt, but you will miss out on helpful bonuses. If you do that, consider picking up some books to read - they can make up the defecit.

We imagine you’re reading this as you’re playing the new re-release of Persona 4 Golden, but as a side note we want to point out that these questions and solutions are completely different to vanilla Persona 4: if you’re playing that back on PS2, this guide won’t help you!

    • Tulip Mania (4th Choice)
  • 4/25 - What’s it called when you gain more muscle after getting sore through exercise?
    • Overcompensation (2nd Choice)
  • 4/26 - Chie: I don’t think I’ve heard of any of these?

  • 4/30 - What is the greatest canyon in the solar system?
